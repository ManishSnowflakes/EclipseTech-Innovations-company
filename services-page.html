Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How long does it take to develop a website?

The timeline for website development varies depending on the complexity of the project. A simple business website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex projects may take 6-12 weeks.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance for websites and apps?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance packages including regular updates, security patches, performance optimization, and technical support.

What platforms do you develop mobile apps for?

We develop native mobile applications for both Android and iOS, and also offer cross-platform solutions using technologies like React Native or Flutter.

How do your digital marketing services work?

Our digital marketing strategy starts with understanding your business goals and target audience, then crafting a customized mix of SEO, PPC, social media, and content marketing to drive results.

What security measures do you implement for websites?

We implement multiple layers of security including SSL certificates, secure authentication, regular updates, firewall protection, and malware scanning. For e-commerce sites, we also ensure PCI DSS compliance.
